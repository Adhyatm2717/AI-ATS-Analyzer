    Pitfalls

    Why ATS Rejects Resumes

    Missing Keywords

    If the system searches for "React" and you only list "Frontend Development", you will likely be skipped.

    Bad Formatting

    Complex layouts, multi-columns, and heavy graphics can completely break older ATS parsing systems.

    No Measurable Impact

    Listing basic responsibilities instead of quantifiable achievements lowers your candidate ranking.

    Algorithm

    How ATS Judges Your Resume

    Keyword Matching

    Scanners look for exact and related phrasing matches between your document and the targeted job responsibilities.

    Experience Relevance

    Total years of experience are calculated by reading job dates and measuring them against the required seniority.

    Skill Density

    Calculated by how frequently the required hard skills appear relative to the total overall length of your resume.

    Solutions

    How To Improve Your Resume

    Add Job Description Keywords

    Mirror the exact job description keywords. Organically integrate them into your summary and experience bullet points.

    Use ATS Friendly Format

    Stick to a single-column layout. Use standard fonts, remove complicated tables, and use recognizable section headers.

    Add Projects & Metrics

    Always showcase the results of your work. Incorporate numbers and data metrics to explicitly prove your competence.
